About

Meet The Aetaconnect

Aetaconnect is managed by board members with a diverse array of skills, collectively having over seventeen years of experience. Offering a Farm-to-Table experience without going through the store or market along the way.

Said Jr. Hussin, the CEO, holds a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology and Business Administration from Bataan Peninsula State University, as well as four years of managerial and retail experience. His responsibility is to expand the organization.

The Chief Operating Officer is Chrislord Dizon. Chrislord is a graduate of the BPSU Custom Administration program and has seven years of experience in operational administration. With knowledge of retail management, he oversees daily operations to ensure that our needs are met.

Aliah Nicole Javier, Chief Financial Officer: Aliah holds a master's degree in accounting from BPSU and has three years of experience in financial management. She oversees financial planning, investments, and projections.

Maria Rafaela Sampang, E-commerce Manager: Rafaela's proficiency in digital marketing is validated by her BPSU degree. She is responsible for the oversight of the firm's platform, including product listings, payment channels, and customer experience.

Jhan Criss Alba, a BPSU information management graduate, has been responsible for marketing analytics for more than three years. He evaluates data in order to render conclusions.

How it Works

STEP 1

We identify Aeta farmers who are interested in collaborating through our website and vice versa.

Step 2

We evaluate the quality of their products to determine if they meet the required standards.

Step 3

Aetaconnect serves as a conduit for Aeta Farmers to market their products to a broader audience.

Step 4

We notify the Aeta Farmers when their product is prepared for harvest.

Step 5

Aetaconnect subsequently ensures that the products arrive at their destination safely.
